3DBIN

3DBin app uses technology that compiles photos of your product from different degrees to create '3D' - spin view of the product. I've collaborated with the product team and engineers to shape product vision and direction. I was working on the UX and UI structure. I've also designed the logo and t-shirts.
Along with Facebook Messenger and Wunderlist, 3DBin was featured on the list of "20 New Best Apps of July, 2014".
