3DBIN

ROLE

Mobile design, Branding

DATES

2013-2014

3DBin app uses technology that compiles photos of your product from different degrees to create '3D' - spin view of the product. I've collaborated with the product team and engineers to shape product vision and direction. I was primarily designing their iOS app. I've also designed the logo and t-shirts.

Along with Facebook Messenger and Wunderlist, ​3DBin was featured on the list of "20 New Best Apps of July, 2014".​​

This page is a short overview of the app. If you want to see more of the process - don't hesitate to ask me.
