SCROLL

Scroll is my personal project. For this project I have collaborated with an amazing iOS developer - Viktor Radchenko. Scroll Feed is an iOS app that lets you discover content based on your interests.

The goal was to make a simple feed with topics that are easy to filter. Viktor and I came up with the idea and I was involved with the design and development of this app since day 1. I was working on user experience, UI structure, identity and other components. For the backend we use Parse. The app is also available for iPad.
