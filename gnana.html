Gnana

ROLE

Web, Mobile design, Branding

DATES

2013-2014

Gnana is a well-funded startup in stealth mode at the intersection of intelligent relationship management, machine learning, and capital optimization. In simple words — it's an analytics tool that helps your company predict its sales. It tracks your data from all your sales points and transactions and combines into simple and digestible format for you.

I joined the small team of developers and data scientists to come up with a simple, and smooth interface. I was improving UX, prototyping and designing UI elements. Later, I was responsible for the design of the corporate identity and some print work.
