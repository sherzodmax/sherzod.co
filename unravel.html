UNRAVEL

I have a passion for building social networking apps. I’ve always been interested in psychology and what drives human behavior. Today, people use their devices very often and I think it's great if they can meet new pople through their devices. I see a big room for imporvment in soical networking space - which was one the major motivations to join Unravel.
I came on board at a very early stage, to build the app basically from the ground up. The team had just come up with a few sketches and rough prototypes.

Role:
Mobile UI/UX Design

Dates:
Sept 2014 - April 2015

Design goals:
1. Design a dating app where the main feature is to unlock someone’s photos by guessing their personality. When two people have unlocked each others' photos - they can chat.
2. Make it user-friendly and visually pleasing.

DESIGN METHODS:
Sketching and storyboarding, wireframing, creating user flows, designing low to high fidelity mocks, conducting user interviews, A/B tests, focus groups, surveys, user behavior data metrics, usability testing.
